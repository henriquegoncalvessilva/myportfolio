Aurora Fight - Beat'em Up




Overview:
Aurora Fight it's a beat 'em up game developed by Tetraedro Studio and inspired by the 80s atmosphere. The political system is corrupted. The city has falling to the edge of oblivion. The chaos and the crime took over the streets of Aurora. The violence has reached its peak, no place is more safer...

Learning Goals:
  • C# Programming
  • Mechanics Study
  • Work in team
  • Project Management
  • Unity Content Design

This project has two people, one programmer and one artist 2D. The game never was release, but anyone can download in page of Itch.io. The time of development this project was 2 moths. I worked in all programation: screens, player, enemys, HUD, etc. I used Unity Engine in project and the language C#. I too helped in player test and social media.

The memories of Uncle Zé - Global Game Jam 2019

Overview:
A gentleman in his golden years wanted to return to his old home so that he could comfort himself with the memories of his life. He seeks to find fragments of photos of his family to help him in this mission.

Learning Goals:
  • C# Programming
  • Work in 48 hours
  • Project Management

This project has one team of five people: Two Programmers, one Artist 2D, one Game Designer and one Sound Designer. The game was release and anyone can download in page OFFICIAL of Global Game Jam 2019.

I worked in programation: screens, player and HUD.
I used Unity Engine in project and the language C#.
