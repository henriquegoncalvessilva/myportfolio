Aurora Fight - Beat'em Up




Overview:
Aurora Fight it's a beat 'em up game developed by Tetraedro Studio and inspired by the 80s atmosphere. The political system is corrupted. The city has falling to the edge of oblivion. The chaos and the crime took over the streets of Aurora. The violence has reached its peak, there is no more safe place...

Learning Goals:
  • C# Programming
  • Mechanics Study
  • Work in team
  • Project Management
  • Unity Content Design

I worked on programming: screens, player, enemies, HUD, etc. Unity Engine was used on the project and was coded with C#. I also helped testing it with the users and social media.This project had two people, one programmer and one 2D artist. The game was never released, but anyone can download it on Itch.io. The development time of this project was 2 months.
